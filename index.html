Barriers to Inclusion

People with disabilities often experience barriers to fully participating in conferences and other events. An attendee using a wheelchair may find that the talk they’ve been looking forward to is up a flight of stairs. Someone with low vision may be unable to sign up for the sessions they want, due to an inaccessible website.

These issues escalate, result in bad word of mouth and loss of future revenue, and sometimes must be resolved in court.

This type of exclusion usually stems from limited knowledge and application of legal obligations, such as the requirements of the Americans with Disabilities Act. Most organizations also lack staff and resources to address access and reasonable accommodation needs before, during, and after events

How our team responds

We help organizations like yours to create inclusive conferences and events. Our team members are experts on accessibility, accommodations, and universal design.

We provide pre-event planning, onsite production, and post-event follow-up. With our help, you will be able to create fully accessible and welcoming environments that engage all individuals.

Assessment and Planning BEFORE your event:

  • Strategic marketing to attract diverse participants and speakers
  • Comprehensive registration tool and process to ensure full access and accommodations for all participants and speakers
  • Accessibility survey and review of legal responsibilities
  • Inclusive program design and preproduction planning
  • "Disability 101" training for event/venue staff
  • Inclusive emergency evacuation/response assistance
  • Accessible transportation, communication, and equipment vendors
  • Data collection

Onsite Support DURING your event:

  • Venue and event accessibility: set-up, monitoring, and quality assurance
  • Ongoing staffing and consultation, from registration to closing
  • On-the-spot troubleshooting and resolution
  • Seamless integration of access and accommodations with event production
  • Ongoing data collection

follow-up and recommendations AFTER your event:

  • Assistance with closing out vendor agreements and rental services
  • Post-event evaluation of satisfaction with access and accommodation details
  • Data analysis and final report, including recommendations for future events

Who We Are

Our team provides disability inclusion services for events, meetings, and conventions. We have assisted organizations on all levels to exceed legal compliance and ensure fully inclusive environments.
